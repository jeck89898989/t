About the Scales Thesaurus

Welcome to the Scales Thesaurus, your comprehensive interactive guide to the world of musical scales, chords, intervals, and music theory. Our mission is to provide musicians of all levels – from curious beginners to seasoned professionals – with powerful, intuitive tools to explore, understand, and apply music theory concepts.

Our Vision

We believe that understanding music theory shouldn't be a barrier to creativity, but a gateway. The Scales Thesaurus was born from a desire to demystify complex musical structures and make learning engaging and practical. We aim to bridge the gap between abstract theory and tangible application on your instrument.

Key Features

  • Interactive Fretboard: Visualize scales, chords, and intervals on a customizable fretboard for various stringed instruments.
  • Extensive Library: Explore hundreds of scales and chords, complete with formulas, spellings, and audio playback.
  • Customization Galore: Tailor the appearance, instrument setup, and display modes to fit your learning style and needs.
  • Pattern Playback: Hear scales and chords played back, helping to connect the visual patterns with the sound.
  • Melodic Patterns: Practice scales using various melodic sequences to improve technique and ear training.
  • Theory Info Panel: Get detailed information about the selected pattern, including quality, related modes, and descriptions.
  • Export & Share: Save diagrams as images or create slideshows to study or share your findings.
  • Filtering & Favorites: Easily find specific scales or chords using interval filters or mark your favorites for quick access.
